== Description ==

Catmandu Child is clean, responsive, modern and feature rich business WordPress theme. It is suitable for business, portfolio, digital agencies and general corporate website. It is designed with great attention to details, flexibility and performance thus looks stunning on all types of screens and devices. Catmandu Child comes with creative and well-organized sections and very extensible and secure code.
